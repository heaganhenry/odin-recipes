Chemical X

Description

A specialty of Professor Utonium. Used for some extra *kick*.

Ingredients

  • Molasses
  • Paprika
  • Everything not nice

Steps

  1. Add 2 cups of molasses to 5 cups of water and stir thoroughly.
  2. Add a pinch of paprika and boil the solution on medium heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Add everything not nice and allow the solution to cool. Use sparingly and whatever you do, do not use this to create little girls!
