Jawbreaker

Description

Ed's go-to jaw breaking jawbreakers.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Coloring
  • Giant candy molds

Steps

  1. Add an exorbitant amount of water and sugar to a pot and stir until the sugar is uniformly moist
  2. Heat the pot on high heat and stir frequently. Monitor until almost all the water is gone.
  3. Take off the heat, add coloring and pour the syrup like solution into the candy molds.
  4. Put the molds into a fridge big enough to fit them and allow to cool for 1 to 2 hours.
